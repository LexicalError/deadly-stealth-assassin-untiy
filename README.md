## Play here

[https://reyuna.itch.io/deadly-stealth-assassin](https://reyuna.itch.io/deadly-stealth-assassin)

## About

This is a Unity assassin game, your goal is to eliminate the target on the map, the minimap will expand as you explore. Enemies outside your camera will **not** see you, you are invisible to all enemies that are outside, any actions you make will thus also be invisible and undetected. Move your camera to your advantage and hide enemies away!

## Controls

- WASD - Move
- Left Mouse - Attack
- Z - Crouch
- Scroll wheel - Swap weapons
- Shit - Run
- E - Interact
- V - Enable minimap
- F - Recenter Camera
- Space + WASD - Move Camera
